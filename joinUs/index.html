Interested in joining our lab?

Anyone interested in the Austerweil Lab should begin by getting to know the research we do. The best resource for this is our most recent publications.

Potential postdocs: Please contact Dr. Austerweil directly.

Potential graduate students: We are not currently taking new grad students at this time.

Potential research assistants: Please send Dr. Austerweil an email with the following details:

  • Name
  • WiscID
  • Year of study
  • Major
  • Why you want to join the lab?
  • What type of projects are you interested in?
Include any relevant research and programming experience.
